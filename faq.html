Frequently Asked Questions

What is NiteSkye Productions?

A relaxed and creative community built for good times, deep talks, and unforgettable nights under virtual skies. Whether you're here to chill, create, dance, or just escape reality for a bit, we've got a spot for you.

Who runs the group?

NiteSkye is the Owner of NSP.
NSP is also managed by a dedicated staff team.

13 is the bare minimum.

No. We are mainly on VRChat, but are not limited to it.
